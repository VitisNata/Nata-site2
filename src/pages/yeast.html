Yeast

The natural microflora of grapes and their processed products is represented by beneficial and harmful yeast groups. Only true wine yeast of the genus Saccharomyces is of practical value for winemaking. Healthy yeast is yeast that turns grape must into good quality wine. These include mainly yeast species Saccharomyces vini and Saccharomyces oviformis. From them, by selection, yeast races (strains) are selected for production, which are then used for the preparation of pure crops.
