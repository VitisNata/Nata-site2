Grape varieties

Each variety has its own unique taste. One gardener will like it, the other will not. To determine which grape variety is best planted in Belarus, it is worth paying attention to the important characteristics of each variety:

  • Frost resistance
  • Disease resistance
  • Precocity
  • Features of care
  • Appointment

    • As a rule, there are 4 grape varieties:

  • Black
  • White
  • Pink
  • Red

    • Click here for more info about this grape varieties.

    Nowadays, grapes have ceased to be something exotic and feel quite good on Belarusian beds. It can be planted both in spring and autumn. But the main thing is to find the right variety, carefully take care of the plant and then the grapes will delight you with a rich harvest.
