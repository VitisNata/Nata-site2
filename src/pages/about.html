About us

Pinsk winery

The history of the plant dates back to the end of the 19th century. In the southernmost point of the country, in the capital of the Belarusian Polesie, a private distillery was founded on the estate of Count Krasitsky. The count's own strong wines were famous for their excellent taste and unique bouquet. Over the course of more than a century of history, the company has managed to preserve the traditions of fruit and berry winemaking.

The first Belarusian vineyard

In 2003, the first Belarusian vineyard was planted at the plant. Currently, industrial grape varieties "Bianka", "Kievsky Bely", "Taiga Emerald", "Alpha" and others are successfully grown on a plot of more than 70 hectares. The plant also has an industrial apple orchard with an area of 22 hectares and plantings of aronia (black-fruited mountain ash) with an area of 11 hectares.

Thanks to modern equipment and a full production cycle, which includes the entire chain from the procurement of raw materials to the release of the final product, JSC Pinsky Winery has a unique opportunity to carry out continuous quality control at all stages of production.

PRODUCTION FACILITIES AND RAW MATERIALS BASE

  • 100 hectares of vineyards and apple orchards
  • 1200 decaliters per day of distilling anhydrous alcohol
  • 168 tons per day of concentrated juice production
